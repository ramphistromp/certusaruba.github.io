A5. External Audit Support

Certus specializes in supporting external audits, lightening the load for your employees and ensuring a smooth process. B1. Internal Audit Function Set-up

We establish a professional internal audit function by developing tailored policies and methodologies aligned with IPPF standards. Our focus is on creating charters and a risk-based audit plan, defining roles and responsibilities, and promoting independence and objectivity. With a strong internal audit structure, we help your organization meet regulatory expectations, enhance governance, and drive continuous improvement.

B2. Execution of Internal Audits

We conduct various audits—compliance, operational, financial, and IT—to assess regulatory adherence, process efficiency, and the integrity of financial systems. Our internal audit services follow IPPF standards, ensuring independence and value-driven outcomes. By identifying risks and control weaknesses, we offer actionable recommendations that enhance governance and strengthen internal controls. 

B3. Risk Assessment

At Certus, we conduct risk assessments to identify and prioritize potential threats to your organization’s objectives. By evaluating financial, operational, compliance, and strategic risks, we help you understand your risk landscape. Our team develops focused audit plans to effectively allocate resources toward critical vulnerabilities. Following IPPF standards and industry best practices, we provide actionable insights and recommendations to strengthen your risk management framework.

B4. Evaluation of Internal Controls

At Certus, we evaluate internal controls to assess their design, implementation, and effectiveness in protecting assets, ensuring compliance, and enhancing operational efficiency. We identify control gaps, such as poor segregation of duties and weak safeguards, and provide recommendations to address these issues. By aligning with existing frameworks and IPPF standards, we help organizations establish robust control environments that mitigate risks.

B5. Pre-scan Quality Assurance Reviews

We perform pre-scan Quality Assurance Reviews (QARs) to prepare your internal audit function for assessments and ensure compliance with IPPF standards. Certus's internal audit services enhance risk management, governance, and internal controls, helping you achieve your strategic goals.
