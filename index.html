About Us

At CERTUS Accountancy, Advisory & Consultancy (CERTUS), we believe that financial clarity and strategic insight are key drivers of organizational success. Based in Aruba, Certus offers a full suite of services across Accounting, Internal Audit, Risk Management, Consultancy, Advisory, and Interim Management. Our dedicated professionals bring together technical knowledge, industry experience, and a genuine commitment to each client’s unique goals.

Certus was founded on the principles of transparency, accountability, and excellence. Whether providing meticulous accounting services, implementing robust risk management practices, or guiding clients through strategic decision-making, our goal is to empower organizations to make confident, informed choices. We work closely with our clients, adapting to their specific challenges and objectives and providing strategic and practical guidance.

Clients choose Certus for our expertise and the personalized, proactive approach we bring to every engagement. Our team takes the time to understand your organization’s values and vision, ensuring that the solutions we provide are truly aligned with your needs. We look forward to building lasting partnerships with clients who value precision, insight, and a shared commitment to sustainable growth.



About Us

Ramphis Tromp

Ramphis E.M. Tromp is a seasoned financial professional and leader with over 18 years of experience in accountancy, advisory services, and internal auditing. His expertise encompasses a range of local industries throughout the Dutch Caribbean. Ramphis adeptly merges technical precision with strategic insight to deliver customized solutions that enable businesses to achieve sustainable growth and operational excellence.

He earned a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Aruba, along with an Accountant-Administratieconsulent (AA) designation from the Koninklijke Nederlandse Beroepsorganisatie van Accountants. Moreover, he holds certifications in Internal Control Risk Analysis (ISO 31000), internal auditing and Information Security (ISO 27001), underscoring his dedication to upholding global best practices in governance, risk management, and compliance.

Starting his career in external auditing, Ramphis progressed through the ranks at prestigious firms such as EY Aruba. Over the years, he has successfully established and led internal audit functions, conducted comprehensive risk assessments, and provided strategic advisory services to a variety of businesses and organizations. His hands-on approach guarantees that clients receive practical and actionable insights tailored to their specific challenges.

With his multifaceted expertise, Ramphis offers clients well-rounded solutions that harmonize compliance, efficiency, and innovation. At Certus, he leads with the guiding principle, "Progress through Sharing," working closely with clients to create robust financial frameworks and drive success.
