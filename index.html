About Us

Ramphis Tromp

Ramphis E.M. Tromp is a seasoned financial professional with over 19 years of experience in accountancy, advisory services, and internal auditing across diverse industries in Aruba and the Dutch Caribbean. Combining technical precision with strategic insight, Ramphis delivers tailored solutions that drive sustainable growth and operational excellence.

He holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Aruba and the prestigious Accountant-Administratieconsulent (AA) designation from the "Koninklijke Nederlandse Beroepsorganisatie van Accountants". Additionally, his certifications in Internal Control Risk Analysis (ISO 31000), Internal Auditing, Information Security (ISO 27001), Corporate Governance, reflect his commitment to global best practices in accountancy, internal auditing. governance, risk management, and compliance.

Ramphis began his career in external auditing and accountancy, advancing through esteemed firms like EY Aruba. Over time, he has built and led internal audit functions. His practical, hands-on approach ensures that clients receive actionable insights aligned with their unique challenges.

Our Services

What We Offer

At Certus, our mission is to empower organizations with precise, insightful, and ethical financial guidance. We enhance transparency, accountability, and resilience in our clients' operations through tailored accountancy, Internal audit, risk management, advisory & consultancy services. By prioritizing trust and excellence, we help clients confidently navigate financial complexities, fostering sustainable growth and long-term success.

Compilation of Financial Statements

Certus specializes in financial statement compilation, ensuring that your business’s financial data is presented accurately and complies with applicable standards.

Review of Financial Statements

Certus offers the service of reviewing financial statements, providing an additional layer of assurance for your business’s financial reporting.

Bookkeeping

Certus provides professional bookkeeping services to ensure your financial records are accurate, organized, and up-to-date.


Financial report for fiscal purposes

Certus provides financial reporting services for fiscal purposes, ensuring your business complies with legal requirements.


Internal Audit Function Set-up

We help establish a professional internal audit function by creating tailored policies, charters, and methodologies, ensuring alignment with IPPF standards.


Risk Assessment

Our team performs risk assessments and develops risk-based audit plans to prioritize high-impact areas and optimize resource utilization.



Executive of Internal Audits

We conduct various audits—compliance, operational, financial, and IT—to evaluate regulatory adherence, process efficiency, and financial and information security integrity.

Evaluation of Internal Controls

We identify and address control gaps, such as insufficient segregation of duties, while providing recommendations to enhance the design and implementation of internal controls.

Quick Scan & GAP Analysis

Certus offers quick scan and GAP analysis services to evaluate your organization or products. The quick scan highlights opportunities and bottlenecks, leading to actionable insights. This can result in a GAP analysis that addresses issues and outlines a roadmap for progress, supporting your growth.

Business Plan & Research

Certus offers professional business plan services designed to help you secure financing, attract investors, or clarify your entrepreneurial strategy. We work collaboratively with you to ensure the plan reflects your goals while minimizing your time commitment, delivering a high-quality document tailored to your needs.

Interim Management

When your organization requires skilled and experienced leadership to navigate planned or unexpected opportunities, Certus is here to assist. Whether it's for a special financial project or to bridge the gap left by a key financial professional, our interim management services provide the expertise you need.

Overall Business Advisory

We partner with clients at various levels, utilizing methodologies to deliver impactful advisory services. Our process emphasizes listening and understanding to align our solutions with client goals.




External Audit Support

Certus specializes in supporting external audits, easing the workload for your employees, and ensuring a smooth process. Our experienced professionals help businesses navigate audit complexities by preparing detailed audit files and financial statements, coordinating with external auditors, and sharing knowledge with your finance team. Partnering with Certus allows your team to focus on their core responsibilities while we manage the audit intricacies with expertise and professionalism.

Pre-scan Quality Assurance Reviews

We conduct pre-scan Quality Assurance Reviews (QARs) to prepare your internal audit function for assessments, ensuring compliance with IPPF standards.
Certus’s internal audit services enhance your risk management, governance, and internal control practices, giving your organization confidence in achieving strategic goals. Whether you need assistance in establishing or refining your audit processes, Certus is here to help.


Strategic Business Planning

At Certus, we believe a Strategic Business Plan is essential for your company’s future, providing clarity and focus for your goals. Whether launching a product, expanding operations, acquiring a business, or improving performance, a strategic plan aligns your team and optimizes resources. We partner with you to develop a tailored plan that evaluates your vision, market position, competitive landscape, leadership structure, financial health, and risks, empowering you to make informed decisions for long-term success.
